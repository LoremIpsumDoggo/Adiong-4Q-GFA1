Wikimedia Foundation

https://wikimediafoundation.org/

I support this charity organization because it is an organization that aims to make educational content available to everyone around the world, according to the website it hosts, Wikipedia. I regularly rely on it when looking up general information on Google, which often uses Wikipedia on its top page. The Wikimedia Foundation also has other products other than the most well-known one, Wikipedia, to promote their mission. One of these other products is Wikimedia Commons, a media repository which stores free images, audio, videos, etc. for anyone to use, including me.
